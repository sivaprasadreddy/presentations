Article Structure

  • Interesting Title
  • First Paragraph : Brief overview of what reader is going to learn in the Article
  • Overview of article: A bulleted list of main sub-headings
  • Assumptions/Preconditions
  • Describe the problem

Article Structure (cont...)

  • Getting started with X
